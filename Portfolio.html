Employment

Utah Department of Corrections

I currently work for the Utah Department of Correction as an Adult Probation and Parole Agent. I supervise offender who have been to jail and/or prison. Most of the offenders on my case load have severe mental ilnessess.

I was born in Thousand Oaks, California. My parents moved us to Bountiful, Utah when I was 4 years old. After a car crashed into the corner of our house we moved to Orem, Utah. This is where I grew up. I attended Orem Suncrest Elementary school, Orem Junior High school, and Mountain View High School.

After high school I went on an LDS mission to Lansing, Michigan. I spent 2 years in Michigan, and moved back to Utah in 2003.

After moving back I enrolledin college and started attending classes to become a firefighter paramedic. I found out rather quickly that school is very expensive, so I picked up a part time job to try to make ends meet, and continue to pay for school.

I also found out that if you wanna go on dates you need money. After that first semester at school I started working full time. I enjoyed spending money so much that I had to pick up a second job.
